About D.A.R.E.

D.A.R.E. (Dave’s Auto Repair & Essentials) was built on a simple idea: bring honest, reliable automotive service directly to the people who need it.

Experience That Matters

With over 20 years of experience in manufacturing, troubleshooting, and operations leadership, D.A.R.E. approaches every vehicle with an industrial mindset: diagnose the root cause, fix it right, and keep it running.

Why Mobile?

Not everyone has time to sit in a shop lobby or deal with towing. Mobile service brings convenience, transparency, and efficiency straight to your driveway, jobsite, or business lot.

Values

• Honest communication
• Practical, cost‑effective solutions
• Respect for your time and budget
• No upselling — ever

Service Area

Based in Northeast Ohio, serving Akron, Canton, Medina, Wooster, Wadsworth, and surrounding communities.
