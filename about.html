About D.A.R.E.

D.A.R.E. (Dave’s Auto Repair & Essentials) is a mobile automotive service dedicated to providing convenient, reliable, and professional maintenance directly at your home. With over 20 years of experience in operations, mechanical troubleshooting, and customer‑focused service, D.A.R.E. was built to make car care easier for Northeast Ohio families.

No shop visits. No waiting rooms. No hassle. We come to you — clean, efficient, and fully prepared.
